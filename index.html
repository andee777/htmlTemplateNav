Are you alarmed by the RAPID DESTRUCTION of our town's natural spaces and wetlands?

On July 7th, New Brunswick city council voted whether or not to sell this unique forest to developers! A plan to protect the Big forest in its entirety - as proposed by Mike Duggan (Peel district councilor) and Maxime Pedneaud-Jobin (Mayor of Greely) - has been unanimously accepted by all members of the Greely municipal council.

The forest WILL NOT BE SOLD to developers. It WILL BE PRESERVED!

Furthermore, propositions have been made to change municipal policies regarding the sale of such valuable natural spaces in the future.

The Big forest needs to be protected in its ENTIRETY to ensure the preservation of its biological, cultural, and archaeological value.
